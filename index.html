Education companies don't fail on content. They fail on infrastructure.

Most education companies were born from pedagogy, not from systems. They have strong courses and teachers — but fragile processes, manual operations, and disconnected tools.

AIFLUENT exists to be the "middle company": the invisible layer that connects LMS, sales, payments and data into one high-performance engine.
